AutoZoom is a one-stop destination for all your Zoom meeting links!

Click on the "Start" Button to view your current meetings. You'll be able to see your daily schedule with the current meeting link active.

To create a new meeting detail, click on the "Add Meetings" button. Then enter your meeting link and time. You can also give the meeting a label, to help sort through your meetings.

The back button will take you back to the home page.
